# DEVELOPMENT OF MUSIC EDUCATIONAL GAME

An important aspect in the world of culture is music. Over the years, the study of this area has improved, moving from teaching from mouth to mouth to direct teaching in special institutions. However, in the 21st century, new learning technologies have appeared. This platform will help solve this problem with a website, by automating the way of learning. The web app is an online ear training tool based on moving Do. It will improve your instrumental music learning by providing real-time feedback on how to play chords as well as recognizing them on any polyphonic instrument such as guitar or piano. You can also log in to the system and watch training video tutorials.
